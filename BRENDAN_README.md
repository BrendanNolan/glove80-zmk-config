# Connection Trouble

If the bluetooth or even USB connections mess up, do a full reset:

1. Take note that the following steps are relative to what is printed on the keys, not what your
   keymap is set to.
2. Forget the `Glove80` in the bluetooth settings of all of your devices.
2. With both halves on, hit `Magic+F10` .
3. Turn both halves off.
4. Hold `PgDn+8` on the right half, then power the right half on while continuing to hold `PgDn+8`
   for `5` seconds.
5. Hold `Magic+3` on the left half, then power the left half on while continuing to hold `Magic+3`
   for `5` seconds.
6. (Not strictly necessary) Reflash the firmware for your keymap.
